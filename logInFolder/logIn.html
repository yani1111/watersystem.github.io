Accessible and clean water is essential for public health, hygiene, and well-being. Reliable water services ensure that everyone has access to safe drinking water, supporting both quality of life and economic growth. However, increasing global population and rising water demand are stressing already limited resources. Combined with climate change and pollution, this threatens water availability. Sustainable water management is key to ensuring equitable distribution now and in the future.
